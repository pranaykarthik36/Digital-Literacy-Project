Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
     Do’s
•	Think before you post - ensure content is appropriate and respectful. 
•	Protect your privacy - use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings. 
•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 
•	Be respectful and polite in comments and interactions. 
•	Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development. 
      Don’ts
•	Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly. 
•	Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior. 
•	Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content. 
•	Don’t believe or forward unverified news or rumors. 
•	Don’t spend excessive time on social media affecting studies or health.
