## Platforms I Chose

I set up profiles on three platforms – GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle.

## What Each Platform is Used For

**GitHub** is a platform where developers store, manage and share 
their code. It acts like a portfolio for programmers. I created 
a profile README that shows my name, branch and what I am 
currently learning.

**LinkedIn** is a professional networking platform used by students, 
working professionals and companies. It is mainly used to showcase 
education, skills and work experience, and to connect with people 
in the industry.

**Kaggle** is a platform focused on data science and machine learning. 
It has datasets, competitions and notebooks that students can use 
to practice and improve their AI/ML skills.

## How I Plan to Use Them Over the Next Four Years

In the first year I am just setting up the basics and getting 
familiar with these platforms. As I progress, I plan to push 
my projects and assignments to GitHub, update my LinkedIn 
profile whenever I complete a course or internship, and use 
Kaggle to practice machine learning once I reach that stage 
in my course. By the time I graduate I want all three profiles 
to actually reflect the work I have done.
